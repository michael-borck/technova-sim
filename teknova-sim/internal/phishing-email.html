Phishing Email Analysis

This is the email that was sent to multiple TechNova staff members on November 15, 2024, at approximately 9:00 AM.

⚠️ Key Indicators of Phishing:

  • From address domain: tecknova.com (missing 'h' - should be technova.com)
  • Link destination: backups-secure.net (not CloudSafe's domain)
  • Urgency tactics: "URGENT" and "expires in 24 hours"
  • Grammar issues: "require your immediate attention"
  • Generic greeting: "Dear Valued Customer" instead of personalized
From: CloudSafe Backup Support <backup-support@tecknova.com>
To: all-staff@technova.example
Date: November 15, 2024 9:02 AM
Subject: URGENT: Cloud Backup Renewal Required - Action Needed

Dear Valued Customer,

This is an urgent notification regarding your CloudSafe Backup subscription for TechNova Systems that require your immediate attention.

Your backup service will expire in 24 hours!

To ensure continuous protection of your critical business data, you must renew your subscription immediately. Failure to renew will result in:

  • Loss of all backup history
  • Inability to restore files
  • Compliance violations
  • Potential data loss

Click below to secure your renewal now:

RENEW YOUR BACKUP NOW

You will need to verify your account credentials to process the renewal. This is for your security.

If you have any questions, please contact our support team immediately at backup-help@cloudsafe-support.net

Best regards,
CloudSafe Backup Team
Protecting Your Data Since 2020

This is an automated message. Please do not reply to this email.

Victim Impact

Sarah Mitchell (Accounts Clerk) clicked the link and entered her credentials on the fake site at approximately 9:12 AM.

The attacker then used her credentials to:

  1. Log into the TechNova file server
  2. Navigate to the Invoices directory
  3. Download 23 customer invoice PDFs
  4. Modify the invoices with different bank account details
  5. Send the modified invoices to customers
